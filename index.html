As the Solution

Our Reason

The reason we chose soaps is because the human population is polluting the ocean and it is severely harming it. So we decided to combat that with our eco-friendly, bio-degradeable soaps.

Fact: There is more plastic than fish in the ocean.

About Our Product

Our safe, vegan-friendly soaps come in a range of colours, scents and fun marine animal shapes! The soap can have a variety of bases including: goats milk, honey, aloe vera and sulphate free. We also have a range of colour and scents for the soaps! Packaging will be completely bio-degradeable as it is made out fo a locally sourced brown paper prooduct and secured with string from local businesses.
